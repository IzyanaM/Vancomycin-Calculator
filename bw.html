Body Weight (BW) Calculator

Calculate BMI, BSA, IBW and Adjusted Body Weight.

Input Parameters

*Required only for Ideal Body Weight & Adjusted Body Weight calculation.

Calculation Results

Body Mass Index (BMI): kg/m²
Body Surface Area (BSA): m²
Ideal Body Weight (IBW) (Devine): kg
Adjusted Body Weight (AdjBW): kg
Formula Used

BMI: Weight (kg) / (Height (m))²

BSA (Mosteller): √((Height (cm) × Weight (kg)) / 3600)

IBW (Devine):
Male: 50 kg + 0.9 kg × (Height (cm) − 152 cm)
Female: 45.5 kg + 0.9 kg × (Height (cm) − 152 cm)

Adjusted Body Weight (AdjBW): IBW + 0.4 × (Actual Weight − IBW)
